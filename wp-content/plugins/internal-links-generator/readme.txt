== Description ==

Looking for the best internal linking plugin for Wordpress? Look no further!

Everyone who involved in SEO knows about link building importance for getting higher rankings in Google, Bing or any other Search Engine. But really good SEOs know that internal linking is something you should not avoid. Look at Wikipedia website, their interlinking is something you need to be jealous about.

Why do you need internal links on your site?
Because it helps you to give more contextual credits to the pages of your site, connecting them with relevant keywords.

Why to use Internal Links Generator for internal linking?

- It is created by SEOs for SEOs
- It has a few cool features you won’t find anywhere else
- It has “noob” level easy setup
- It works

What cool features Internal Links Generator has?

- It counts how many times keywords from you list is found in your content (so you will what keywords from the list your content is lacking).
- You can set the limit for keyword usage in your internal links (keyword priority setup, we all know that keywords are not equal).
- You prepare everything in your beloved spreadsheets, then just import it! 
- You are able to find the keywords you want to link within the content of your website.
- You can manually set how many words to look for before and after your keyword, which can help you find not only particular keywords but the surrounding words as well. That means that you can create a lot more variations of your anchor texts (remember: Google loves diversification in anchor texts).

== Installation ==

1. Unzip the archive on your computer  
2. Upload `tidekey` directory to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
4. Go to the `Settings` -> `Internal Links Generator` and set the settings you want.
